Constraints:

Similar to the dispatch formulation, but considering a binary variable $u_t \in \{0, 1\}$ with semi continuous constraints for both active and reactive power:

\[\begin{aligned} & \sum_{t = 1}^N P_t \cdot \Delta T \le E^\text{budget} \\ & P_t - u_t P^\text{max} \le 0 \\ & P_t - u_t P^\text{min} \ge 0 \\ & Q_t - u_t Q^\text{max} \le 0 \\ & Q_t - u_t Q^\text{min} \ge 0 \end{aligned}\]
